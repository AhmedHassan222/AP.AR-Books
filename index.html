FAQ

What is APARBooks?

Aparbooks is a comprehensive accounting software designed to seamlessly integrate accounts payable and accounts receivable. This streamlines your accounting processes, making it easier for every role involved in construction projects, including General Contractors, Project Owners, and Project Managers, to work collaboratively on the same page.

What makes APARBooks unique in the market?

Aparbooks is unique because it's exclusively built for the construction industry, addressing its specific accounting needs, such as change orders and retainage. While offering core accounting functions, our software is affordable for this specialized industry. For pricing details, please visit our product page.

How can APARBooks benefits my business?

Aparbooks is a valuable asset to your construction business, offering cost reduction, time savings, streamlined paperwork management, and user-friendly accounting solutions. By using Aparbooks, you can significantly cut costs, save time on financial management tasks, simplify paperwork procedures, and make the accounting processes easier for your team, ultimately enhancing the efficiency of your construction projects.

What construction industries does APARBooks serve best?

Aparbooks serves a wide range of construction industries, including residential, commercial, and multifamily sectors. Our software is designed to accommodate common construction contract types, making it a versatile solution for various construction projects across these industry segments.

Is it suitable for my project?

Aparbooks is purpose-built to cater to small to mid-sized construction projects, typically with contract prices ranging from $100K to $5M. We have meticulously considered the unique requirements and characteristics of projects falling within this range, ensuring that our software is optimized to deliver exceptional value and efficiency for these specific project sizes.

Is it easy to use?

Aparbooks is designed with user-friendliness in mind. Our intuitive interface and user-centric features ensure that both novices and experienced users can easily navigate and leverage the software. And our support team is available to assist whenever needed.

Can I integrate APARBooks with other construction management tools?

No, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of your company's valuable data. Therefore, we do not integrate Aparbooks with external construction management tools. We have designed Aparbooks to provide comprehensive features that cover the majority of your daily tasks, ensuring efficiency and data protection. Avoiding multiple integrations also helps maintain clean records and minimizes the learning curve, making your accounting processes with Aparbooks as straightforward and secure as possible.

Is my financial data secure?

Your data privacy is our top priority. Aparbooks takes the security of your financial data very seriously. We implement seven layers of encryption, making it virtually impossible for anyone, including us, to access your financial data without your explicit permission.
