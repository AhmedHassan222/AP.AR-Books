Our Histories

2016.12

Begin the idea

In December 2016, the journey to revolutionize construction accounting software began. It all started with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a specialized accounting solution tailored exclusively for the construction industry. Frustration with generic accounting software that couldn't meet the unique demands of project owners and contractors led to the inception of APARBooks

2017.04

Prototype

We achieved a significant milestone by creating our first prototype. This prototype represented the first tangible manifestation of our vision. It allowed us to turn our ideas into a functional model, demonstrating the potential of what APARBooks could offer to project owners and contractors in the construction sector.

2018.06

Production Lunch

With our product now in production, we made our software accessible to project owners and contractors who were seeking an industry-focused, user-friendly, and efficient accounting solution. This milestone not only marked the realization of our vision but also the beginning of our ongoing commitment to support our clients, ensure their success, and continually enhance our software to meet their evolving needs.
