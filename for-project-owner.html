FAQ

How Can APARBooks help me?

As a Project Owner, Aparbooks empowers you to collaborate seamlessly with General Contractors, enhancing communication and project management. You can effortlessly maintain accurate records, ensuring that every financial aspect of your project is meticulously tracked. The data portal brings together all the information you need, offering real-time insights and making informed decision-making a breeze. Aparbooks simplifies the complexities of financial oversight and project management, ultimately ensuring the success of your construction projects.

Is it always Free or just within certain period?

Aparbooks offers a perpetual free subscription plan for Project Owners. You can enjoy the benefits of our software without any time limitations or hidden costs.

How you make profit if your product is free?

While Aparbooks offers a free plan for Project Owners, the General Contractor is required to subscribe and pay for their usage of Aparbooks..

How long can i access my data?

We prioritize the long-term accessibility of your data. Your data is securely stored and will be retained indefinitely, allowing you to access it at any time.

Can I invite my colleague?

Aparbooks is designed as a central platform that encourages collaboration and teamwork. You can easily invite and involve your colleagues, providing them with access to the platform. This ensures that every role involved in your project can work together seamlessly, enhancing communication and efficiency within your project team.

Can I request a demo?

Yes, you can request a demo to experience Aparbooks firsthand. Simply visit our "Request a Demo" page, and choose a suitable time for your demo session.

Does Aparbooks handle Change Orders and Retainage?

Yes, and that's where Aparbooks truly excels. We specialize in handling Change Orders and Retainage, offering unique features tailored specifically for the construction industry.
